DistortionSphere QML Type

A sphere distortion effect. More...

Properties

Detailed Description

The DistortionSphere effect creates a 3D effect of wrapping the scene around a spherical shape.

Property Documentation

center : vector2d

The focus point of the distortion. Qt.vector2d(0.5, 0.5) (i.e. the center of the scene) by default.

distortionHeight : real

The distortion amount. Range is [-1...1. 0.5 by default.

radius : real

The area of distortion. Range is [0...1]. 0.25 by default.
