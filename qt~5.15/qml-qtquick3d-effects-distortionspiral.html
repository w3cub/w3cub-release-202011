DistortionSpiral QML Type

A spiral distortion effect. More...

Properties

  • center : vector2d

Detailed Description

The DistortionSpiral effect creates a twirl-shaped distortion.

Property Documentation

center : vector2d

The focus point of the distortion. Qt.vector2d(0.5, 0.5) (i.e. the center of the scene) by default.

distortionStrength : real

The distortion amount. Range is [-10...10. 1.0 by default.

radius : real

The area of distortion. Range is [0...1]. 0.25 by default.
